How it works  
• The ancilla photons interfere with the four inputs in a carefully optimized beam-splitter network.  
• Certain four-click patterns (e.g. clicks on detectors {4,5}) will only occur if the input is GHZ₄, while a disjoint set of patterns (e.g. detectors {6,7}) only occur for Bell⊗Bell.

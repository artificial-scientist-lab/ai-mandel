Explanation:  
We engineer a three-qubit network primitive that coherently routes an optical data qubit and a microwave data qubit under the control of a single-photon optical qubit—effectively a post-selected Fredkin gate bridging continuous-wave SPDC optical links and microwave-photonic links.

Behavior:

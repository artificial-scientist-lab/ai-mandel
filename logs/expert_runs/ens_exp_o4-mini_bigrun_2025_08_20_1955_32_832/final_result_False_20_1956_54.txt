}###Title: PT-Symmetric Entanglement Swapping via Non-Hermitian Bell-State Projection

Abstract: We implement entanglement swapping on two polarization-encoded Bell pairs using a central PT-symmetric, non-unitary Bell-state analyser. By heralding the projector onto the PT-modified Bell state (the target map) and tuning its gain/loss parameter γ, the protocol controllably generates a two-photon output |Ψ_AD⟩∝|HH⟩+γ e^{iϕ}|VV⟩ with adjustable, non-maximal entanglement.

Title: Photonic SWAP-Test Network for Direct Measurement of Quadratic Fermion Entanglement Spectrum

Abstract: We propose a static linear-optical network whose target transformation is the global SWAP operator enacted across two N-mode photonic registers encoding identical copies of a quadratic fermionic ground state, realized via parallel Bell-pair ancillas and entanglement-swapping modules.
